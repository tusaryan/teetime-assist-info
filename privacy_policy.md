## 1. Our Privacy-First Commitment

Unlike traditional applications, **TeeTime Assist operates 100% offline**.
*   **Zero Internet Permission**: The Application requests absolutely **NO network (internet) permissions**.
*   **No Data Transmission**: Because there is no internet access, it is technically impossible for the Application to transmit, leak, or share any of your settings, coordinates, or inputs to any external server, third party, or database.
*   **Zero Ads & Tracking**: The Application does not display advertisements, does not contain tracking pixels, and does not integrate any third-party marketing or tracking SDKs.

## 2. Permissions We Request & Why

TeeTime Assist requires specific system permissions to function as an assistive hardware simulation utility. All permissions are processed entirely on your local device:

### **A. Accessibility Service API (`AccessibilityService`)**
*   **Purpose**: The Application utilizes the `AccessibilityService` API to perform global system actions on behalf of the user, such as simulating physical clicks of the **Power Key** (locking the screen, showing the power options menu) and adjusting system **Volume Levels**.
*   **No Collection**: The Accessibility Service operates in a strict local sandbox. It **does not** monitor, read, or record your keystrokes, on-screen text, messages, passwords, credit card numbers, or overlay security screens.
*   **Opt-In**: This service is disabled by default. It is only activated when you explicitly grant the permission in your device's Accessibility Settings.

### **B. Display Over Other Apps (System Alert Window)**
*   **Purpose**: This permission is used to display the floating menu drawer and the custom screen dimmer overlay.
*   **Security**: Settings interfaces filter out obscured touches (Tapjacking protection) to ensure malicious overlays cannot hijack permission boxes on your device.

### **C. Do Not Disturb (DND) Access**
*   **Purpose**: This permission is requested only if you choose to control the device's ringer modes (e.g., toggling silent or vibrate) directly from the floating menu drawer.

## 3. Data We Store Locally

TeeTime Assist collects **no personal identification information** (such as names, phone numbers, email addresses, or location data).

*   **Local Configuration Storage**: Your app settings (such as screen dimmer opacity, custom button coordinates, theme colors, and layout configurations) are stored locally on your device's internal storage using Android **Jetpack DataStore**.
*   **Data Deletion**: When you uninstall the Application, all locally stored configuration files are automatically deleted by the Android operating system.
